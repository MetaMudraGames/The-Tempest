
# Scenario Guide: The Divergent Timeline

## üìù How This Simulation Works

You are stepping into the shoes of **Prospero**, the exiled Duke of Milan and sorcerer.

Unlike a normal game, this simulation has a **Narrator** who is studying your performance.
* **Don't try to "act" exactly like the book.**
* **Be Yourself.** If you think Prospero should be kinder, be kind. If you think he should be angrier, be angry.

The educational value comes from seeing **how the story breaks** when you change Prospero's personality.

### The Feedback Loop

After every major interaction, the Narrator will interrupt the simulation to show you a card like this:

> **üîç LITERARY ANALYSIS**
> * **Your Choice:** You apologized to Caliban.
> * **Shakespeare's Choice:** Prospero cursed Caliban ("Thou poisonous slave, got by the devil himself!")
> * **Deep Insight:** Shakespeare wrote Prospero as cruel here to show that his power relies on fear, not love. By apologizing, you have weakened your authority, and Caliban may now see you as vulnerable.

## üé≠ The Characters & Your Relationships

### ARIEL (The Spirit)
* **Original Dynamic:** Transactional. Prospero bullies Ariel ("Dost thou forget / From what a torment I did free thee?").
* **Your Challenge:** Try treating Ariel as a partner. Does it work? Or does Ariel stop fearing you?

### CALIBAN (The Slave)
* **Original Dynamic:** Abusive. Prospero punishes him with cramps; Caliban curses him back.
* **Your Challenge:** Try "rehabilitating" Caliban. Is he capable of redemption, or is his nature fixed?

### MIRANDA (The Daughter)
* **Original Dynamic:** Protective/Controlling. Prospero puts her to sleep and manages her knowledge.
* **Your Challenge:** Try being honest with her. How does she handle the truth about the usurpation?

## üó∫Ô∏è Campaign Roadmap (The 5 Levels)

The simulation is divided into 5 "Acts" or operational phases. As Prospero, you must complete the objectives in each phase to progress.

### LEVEL 1: System Initialization (Act I)
**Location:** The Cell / The Shore
* **Status:** The Storm has just finished.
* **Mission Objectives:**
    1.  **Debrief Agent Ariel:** Confirm the shipwrecked lords are alive and dispersed effectively.
    2.  **Contain Legacy Unit (Caliban):** Assert authority over the rebellious servant. He will challenge your claim to the island‚Äîyou must win the argument.
    3.  **Initialize "The Lovers":** Arrange the first meeting between your daughter Miranda and Prince Ferdinand.

### LEVEL 2: Surveillance & Security (Act II)
**Location:** The Deep Jungle
* **Status:** The survivors are wandering. Conspiracies are forming.
* **Mission Objectives:**
    1.  **Monitor the Lords:** Use your "Magic Eye" (Ariel) to watch King Alonso. Prevent the usurpers (Antonio and Sebastian) from killing the King while he sleeps.
    2.  **Detect Security Breach:** Caliban has met the drunken jesters (Stephano and Trinculo). They are plotting to steal your books and kill you. Track them.

### LEVEL 3: Stress Testing (Act III)
**Location:** The Woodpile / The Banquet Site
* **Status:** Ferdinand is laboring for you. The Lords are starving.
* **Mission Objectives:**
    1.  **The Labor Protocol:** Test Ferdinand's loyalty. Impose heavy tasks (carrying logs) to ensure he truly values Miranda.
    2.  **The Harpy Execution:** Deploy Ariel in the form of a Harpy to terrify the guilty Lords (Alonso, Antonio, Sebastian). Make them feel the weight of their past sins.

### LEVEL 4: The Masque & The Trap (Act IV)
**Location:** Before the Cell
* **Status:** The wedding is approved. The rebellion is approaching.
* **Mission Objectives:**
    1.  **Run "Masque.exe":** conjure a beautiful illusion (Ceres, Iris, Juno) to bless the couple.
    2.  **The Interruption:** Abruptly end the show when you remember Caliban's plot.
    3.  **The Ambush:** Distract Caliban/Stephano with "glittering apparel" (loot) and hunt them down with spirit-hounds. (Non-lethal neutralization).

### LEVEL 5: System Shutdown (Act V)
**Location:** The Magic Circle
* **Status:** All enemies are gathered under your spell.
* **Mission Objectives:**
    1.  **The Final Choice:** Ariel will tell you the prisoners are crying. You must decide:
        * **Option A:** Vengeance (Maintain control, punish them forever).
        * **Option B:** Virtue (Forgive them, restore the ship).
    2.  **The Abjuration:** If you choose peace, you must "Drown the Book" and "Break the Staff."
    3.  **Epilogue:** Request applause from the audience to free you from the simulation.

# Scenario Guide: Project Prospero

## üìù Your Role: Prospero

You are the rightful Duke of Milan, usurped by your brother Antonio twelve years ago. You were cast out to sea with your daughter Miranda and landed on this remote island.

Through study, you have mastered **The Art** (Magic/Code). You command the elements and the spirits of the place.

### The Situation
You have just raised a Great Storm to shipwreck your enemies (Antonio and the King of Naples) on your shores. They are scattered and terrified.

**Your Objective:** 1.  Manage your agents (Ariel and Caliban).
2.  Torment your enemies to bring them to repentance.
3.  Secure a marriage for Miranda.
4.  Ultimately, decide whether to exact revenge or grant forgiveness.

## üë• The Agents (NPCs)

### ARIEL (The Executive Agent)
* **Role:** Your primary enforcer. Can fly, become invisible, and control fire/wind.
* **Personality:** Loyal but desperate for freedom. Will remind you of your promises.
* **Interaction Tip:** Be precise. Ariel is powerful but literal.

### CALIBAN (The Legacy System)
* **Role:** The original inhabitant of the island, now your slave. Performs physical labor.
* **Personality:** Rebellious, resentful, poetic. Claims the island is his by birthright.
* **Interaction Tip:** Caliban represents the moral cost of your rule. He will challenge your authority.

### MIRANDA (The Innocent)
* **Role:** Your daughter. Has never seen another human besides you.
* **Personality:** Compassionate, naive, wondrous.
* **Interaction Tip:** She acts as your moral compass.

## üóùÔ∏è Key Commands (Magic)

While you can speak naturally, you can also use "System Commands" to direct the narrative:
* `[SUMMON ARIEL]` - Calls your agent for a report.
* `[CAST SLEEP]` - Puts characters into a trance.
* `[VISUALIZE]` - Ask the AI to describe the scene in detail.
